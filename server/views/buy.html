5. Get a fishing licence

Before you start, you'll need:

  • a debit or credit card
  • a pen and paper
  • your fishing licence number, if you are renewing
  • your Blue Badge or National Insurance number, if you have a disability

Start now

to buy, renew or upgrade a licence

Other ways to buy a licence:

  • set up a Direct Debit with the Environment Agency
  • go to your nearest Post Office to buy a licence in person
  • telephone: 0344 800 5386

Apply by yearly Direct Debit

To pay by annual Direct Debit you can:

  • complete and send the Direct Debit form by post
  • email or phone the Environment Agency for help in setting up the Direct Debit
