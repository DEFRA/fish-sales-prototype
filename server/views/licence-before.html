Which is right for me?

Buy a new licence

12-month licences - from £30.00

12-month licences are valid for 365 days from their start date and can be purchased at any time during the year.

8-day licences - from £12.00

8-day licences are valid for 8 consecutive days.

1-day licences - from £6.00

1-day licences are valid for a full 24 hours from the start time that you select.

Upgrade a licence

1-day or 8-day licences to 12-month licences

You can upgrade your current 1-day or 8-day licence to the corresponding 12-month licence within 14 days of its expiry.

Trout and coarse licences to salmon and sea trout licences

You can upgrade your 12-month trout and coarse licence to a 12-month salmon and sea trout licence at any time.

2-rod trout licences to 3-rod licences

You can upgrade your 2-rod 12-month trout and coarse licence to a 3-rod 12-month trout and coarse licence at any time
