4. Important changes to fishing licences - coming in April 2017

12-month licence start dates

12-month licences will be valid for a full year from their start date. They can be purchased at any time during the year.

1-day and 8-day licence start times

1-day and 8-day licences will only become valid 30 minutes after payment.

Licence upgrades

You will be able to upgrade your 1-day or 8-day licence to a 12-month licence. When you make this upgrade, your fee will be discounted from the 12-month licence price. You'll have 14 days from the date and time of expiry to complete this upgrade.

3-rod licences for trout and coarse fish

You will be able to buy one licence that allows you to fish with 3 rods.
