4. Important changes to fishing licences

From 1 April 2017, the fishing licence service is introducing several changes.

Free junior licences

If you're between 12 and 16, you'll no longer need to pay for your licence. However, you still need to get a licence, so you will have to go through the online process of getting it.

12-month licences available at any time of the year

12-month licences will now be valid for a full year from their start date. You'll be able to purchase them at any time during the year.

Licence upgrades

You'll be able to upgrade your 1-day or 8-day licence to a 12-month licence. When you make this upgrade, your fee will be discounted from the 12-month licence price. You'll have 14 days from the date and time of expiry to complete this upgrade.

3-rod licences for trout and coarse fish

You'll be able to buy one licence that allows you to fish with 3 rods.

1-day and 8-day licence start times

1-day and 8-day licences will only become valid 30 minutes after payment.
