Before you go fishing

We don't send you a licence in the post. When our bailiffs ask to see your licence, you must show them:

  1. proof of your licence: print out this page or make a note of your licence number
  2. proof of your identity, such as your driver's licence or passport

You must check the rod fishing rules and byelaws before you go.

Your payment

You paid by debit or credit card.

We use your fishing licence money to:

  • encourage people to take-up fishing
  • improve facilities for anglers
  • rear coarse fish for restocking
  • protect stocks from illegal fishing
  • get rid of invasive species

Read the annual fisheries report for more detail about how we spend this money.
