How does my licence money get spent?

We use your fishing licence money to:

  • improve facilities for anglers and habitats for fish
  • protect fisheries from illegal activity
  • prevent the spread of fish diseases and invasive non-native species
  • rear and stock coarse fish to boost natural stocks
  • encourage more people to go fishing

Read the annual fisheries report for more detail about how we spend this money.

Concessions

You can get a disabled concession if you’ve got a Blue Badge, get Disability Living Allowance or Personal Independence Payment (any rate).

You can get a senior concession if you are over the age of 65 on the date of purchase.
