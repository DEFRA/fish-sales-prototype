2. Licence costs

These prices are all valid from 1 April 2017.

I want to buy a licence that starts before 1 April 2017

Up until 1 April 2017, these prices still apply for 1-day and 8-day licences:

  • a 1-day trout and coarse licence is £3.75
  • a 1-day salmon and sea trout licence is £8.00
  • an 8-day trout and coarse licence is £10.00
  • an 8-day salmon and sea trout is £23.00

You can buy a 12-month licence before 1 April 2017, however it will only be valid after this date.

How does my licence money get spent?

We use your fishing licence money to:

  • improve facilities for anglers and habitats for fish
  • protect fisheries from illegal activity
  • prevent the spread of fish diseases and invasive non-native species
  • rear and stock coarse fish to boost natural stocks
  • encourage more people to go fishing

Read the annual fisheries report for more detail about how we spend this money.

Concessions

You can get a disabled concession if you’ve got a Blue Badge, get Disability Living Allowance or Personal Independence Payment (any rate).

You can get a senior concession if you're over the age of 65 on the date of purchase.
