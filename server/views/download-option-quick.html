Which fish are covered by the trout and coarse licence?

A trout and coarse fish licence allows you to fish for freshwater fish such as Carp, Pike and Perch.

Which fishing licence do I need?

A trout and coarse fish licence allows you to fish for freshwater fish such as Carp, Pike and Perch.

A Salmon and sea trout licence allows you to fish for salmon and sea trout as well as all the freshwater fish included in the trout and coarse licence.

Which fishing licence do I need?

A trout and coarse fish licence allows you to fish for all freshwater fish except Salmon and Sea Trout.

A Salmon and sea trout licence allows you to fish for salmon and sea trout as well as all freshwater fish included in the trout and coarse licence.
