Licences for children aged between 13 and 16 are free.

1-day licences are valid for a full 24 hours from the start time that you select.

8-day licences are valid for 8 consecutive days from the start time that you select.

12-month licences are now valid for 365 days from their start date and can be purchased at any time during the year.
